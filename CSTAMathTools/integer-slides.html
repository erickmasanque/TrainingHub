🔄 Subtraction = Add the Opposite

The Golden Rule of Subtraction

a − b = a + (−b)

Change subtraction to addition, then flip the sign of the second number.

BEFORE

(+5) − (+3)

AFTER (Rewrite!)

(+5) + (−3)

Now use the addition rules you already know! 🎯

📋 The Rules We Discovered

Adding: Same Sign

Add the absolute values, keep the common sign.

(+3) + (+2) = +5   |   (−4) + (−3) = −7

Adding: Different Signs

Subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger. Take the sign of the larger absolute value.

(+7) + (−3) = +4   |   (−9) + (+5) = −4

Subtracting Integers

Change subtraction to addition and flip the sign of the second number. Then follow the addition rules!

a − b = a + (−b)   |   (−3) − (−5) = (−3) + (+5) = +2
